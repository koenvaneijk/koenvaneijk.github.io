Featured Projects: Building Autonomous AI Systems

DataTalk

Pioneering AI-Powered Solutions. DataTalk is a technology venture building specialized AI products for various industries. We are the creators of CalCount, RedactWise, and PatGrid.

Our Products

PatGrid: As Co-Founder and technical lead, I am architecting a multi-agent system designed for complex intellectual property analysis.

  • Architecture: Designed a scalable framework where autonomous AI agents collaborate to perform patent searches, analyze claims, and generate reports on patentability and freedom-to-operate.
  • Technology Stack: Implemented using Python, LangChain, and LLMs, featuring a sophisticated Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline to query and reason over millions of technical documents. Deployed on a scalable cloud infrastructure.

RedactWise: I engineered an advanced AI redaction platform for legal and sensitive documents.

  • Core Technology: Developed and fine-tuned specialized computer vision and NLP models for high-precision detection and redaction of sensitive information, including PII and signatures in PDF and Word documents.
  • Impact: The system preserves original document formatting and integrity while ensuring compliance and data privacy, critical for legal and enterprise workflows.

CalCount: I led the development of an AI-powered nutrition monitoring system to improve patient care in healthcare settings.

  • AI System: Built a multi-modal AI that processes patient-submitted photos and text logs. It leverages computer vision for food item recognition and NLP for analyzing textual descriptions to provide accurate, real-time nutritional data.
  • Value Proposition: This automation reduces manual data entry and analysis for healthcare professionals by up to 70%, allowing them to focus on patient outcomes.
